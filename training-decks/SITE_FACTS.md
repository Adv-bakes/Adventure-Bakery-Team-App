# SITE_FACTS — Adventure Bakery (read before authoring/editing any SQF module)

Single source of truth for site-specific reality, so decks match the actual plant
and we don't burn a re-import fixing a wrong assumption. **Read this once at the start
of every module.** If a fact here is wrong or you learn a new one, update this file.

Last reviewed: 2026-06-15.

## The business
- B2B **contract manufacturer** (co-manufacturer), **not** a retail/storefront bakery.
  Imagery is industrial only: production lines, hairnets/bouffant caps, shipping —
  never storefronts, display cases, or chef hats.
- Clients own the products; we make them. Wording rule: never "our [product]" — use
  "the [product] we make" / ES "que fabricamos". Never name customers/retailers/product brands.
- Audited to **SQF Edition 10**.

## People & access (confirmed 2026-06-15)
- **Small team — a handful of employees.** Everyone knows everyone, so an unfamiliar
  face stands out. Lean on this in food-defense/security content.
- **No badge access and no badges worn. No security guards.** Do NOT write about badge
  readers, lanyards, tailgating, badging in, or guards.
- Visitors are vetted at a **reception area** (sign-in, then escorted).
- **Restricted areas are clearly marked with signage** ("Authorized Personnel Only").
  Doors to restricted/exterior areas are kept secured.
- Departments: Production · Sourcing · Quality Control · Admin · R&D · Sales.

## Records & audits (confirmed 2026-06-15)
- Records are kept **mostly on paper** — floor logs and forms (temperature logs,
  cleaning/sanitation records, receiving sheets, CCP/metal-detection checks), filed and
  stored. Examples should reference legible handwriting, real-time entry, initialing/dating,
  no blank gaps, real values.
- **Management/the owner leads the SQF audit** and answers program-level questions. Floor
  staff may be asked direct questions and should answer honestly about their **own job**,
  and defer specifics to their supervisor ("I'll check with my supervisor").

## Products & key materials
- Product lines referenced generically: **rum cakes, biscotti, plant-based/vegan burgers**.
- The plant-based burger carries an **allergen-free ("free-from") claim** — protecting
  that claim is a recurring theme (Modules 3, 4, and the food-fraud tie-in in 11).
- **Rum** is a controlled, high-value ingredient — received, stored, and used **under
  lock and log**. It's the obvious food-defense/theft target; reference it that way.
